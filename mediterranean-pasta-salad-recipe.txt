Ingredients:
 - 2 cups dry tri-color rotini pasta, uncooked
 - 1 small green bell pepper, seeded, thinly sliced
 - 1 cup sliced ripe balck olives
 - 2 ounces Italian dry salami, thinly sliced
 - 2 ounces thin-sliced lean ham
 - 1 can (14.5 oz each) Hunt'sÂ® Fire Roasted Diced Tomatoes, drained
 - 1/3 cup chopped pepperoncini peppers
 - 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette dressing
 - 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
 - 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
 1. Boil the pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until they are
    slightly crunchy, about 10 to 12 minutes.
 
 2. Drain the pasta and chill until cold.
 
 3. Add the chilled pasta to a large serving bowl along with remaining
    ingredients and mix until combined.

 4. Chill before serving.
